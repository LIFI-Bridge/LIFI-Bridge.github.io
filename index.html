How It Works

One Integration. Every Chain.

LI.FI aggregates bridges and DEXs, finds the optimal route, and handles execution — all through a single SDK call.

🔍

Smart Route Discovery

LI.FI scans 20+ bridges and 20+ DEX aggregators to find the best path for your swap — optimizing for price, speed, and reliability.

⇄

Any-to-Any Execution

Swap any token on any chain to any other token on any chain. Single transaction, no manual bridging steps. Works for stablecoins, ETH, RWAs, and more.

🛡

Auto-Failover

If a bridge goes down or experiences congestion, LI.FI automatically reroutes through alternatives. Your app stays live even when bridges don't.
